YAYT

Yet Another YouTube App for iOS

Get the Shortcut! Go to YouTube

Features

  • Standalone App
  • Fullscreen
  • PiP (Picture-in-Picture)
  • Background play
  • Coming soon: no ads

Tutorial

  1. Click "Get the Shortcut!"
  2. Click "Go to YouTube"
  3. Open share sheet and click on "Start YAYT"
  4. Open share sheet and click on "Add to Homescreen"
  5. Open YouTube from Homescreen, open share sheet and click on "Start YAYT"
  6. Each time you reopen YouTube and the YouTube icon is not black, repeat step 5
